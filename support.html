Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Open the DEBARREL app and tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen. Enter your email address, create a password, and follow the prompts to set up your profile. You'll be ready to start tracking your fitness journey in minutes!
How do I log a workout?
Navigate to the "Training" tab, tap the "+" button, and select your exercises. You can log sets, reps, and weights for each exercise. Your workout history is automatically saved and tracked over time.
How do I track my nutrition?
Go to the "Nutrition" tab and tap "Add Meal." Enter your food items and their nutritional information. The app will automatically calculate your daily macros (protein, carbs, fats) and calories.
Can I track my body measurements?
Yes! Visit the "Body Stats" section to log your weight, body fat percentage, and various body measurements. You can view your progress over time with interactive charts.
How do I reset my password?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Check your spam folder if you don't see the email within a few minutes.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your personal information, workout data, and progress photos are stored securely and never shared with third parties without your consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
Can I export my data?
Yes! You can export your workout history, nutrition logs, and body stats from the app settings. This allows you to keep a backup of your data or transfer it to another service if needed.

The app is crashing or not working properly. What should I do?
First, try closing and reopening the app. If that doesn't work, try restarting your device. Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed. If problems persist, please contact us at support@debarrel.app with details about your device model and the issue you're experiencing.
Do you offer a premium version?
Currently, all features of DEBARREL are available to all users. We're constantly working on new features and improvements based on user feedback!
